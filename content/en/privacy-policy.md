### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on our Service and store certain info. The tracking tech used include beacons, tags, and scripts to collect and track info, and to improve and analyze our Service. The tech we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file placed on your Device. You can tell your browser to say no to all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But, if you don't accept Cookies, you might not be able to use some parts of our Service. Unless you've set your browser to give Cookies the cold shoulder, our Service might still use them.
- **Web Beacons**: Certain sections of our Service and our emails might contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also called clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These let the Company, for example, count users who have visited those pages or opened an email and for other related website stats (like checking how popular a certain section is and making sure the system and server are running smoothly).

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stay on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while Session Cookies get wiped out as soon as you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the purposes listed below:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super important to give you services available through the Website and to let you use some of its features. They help authenticate users and stop fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for can't be provided, and we only use these Cookies to give you those services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies figure out if users have given the green light to the use of cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember choices you make when you use the Website, like remembering your login details or language preference. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you from having to re-enter your preferences every time you use the Website.

For more details about the Cookies we use and your choices regarding Cookies, please hit up our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
