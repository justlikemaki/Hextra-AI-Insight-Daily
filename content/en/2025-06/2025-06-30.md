### Cutting-Edge AI Research

1.  The dream team at **CMU** and **Xiaohongshu** just dropped a game-changer: **HoPE** (**Hybrid of Position Embedding**) **mixed position encoding**! 🚀 They found that existing **multimodal RoPE** kinda struggles with "keeping up" when it comes to **long-context semantic modeling**. So, HoPE cleverly brings in **zero-frequency temporal modeling** and **dynamic scaling** strategies. Think of it like giving **Visual Language Models** (**VLMs**) a pair of "marathon shoes"—it seriously amps up their **length generalization** in **long video understanding** and **retrieval** tasks, pushing them straight to peak performance! 💡 How cool is that?! ['Paper Link'](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2505.20444) ['Project Link'](https://github.com/hrlics/HoPE)

2.  Mind blown! The teams from **Renmin University of China** and **Shanghai AI Lab** just dropped a fresh breakthrough—the **MokA** (**Multimodal low-rank Adaptation**) method! 🤯 They found that when fine-tuning **Multimodal Large Language Models** (**MLLMs**), it's easy to lose balance, often overlooking the sweet spot between **independent unimodal modeling** and **inter-modal interaction**. But MokA's like a balancing act pro! By cleverly combining **modality-specific A matrices**, **cross-modal attention mechanisms**, and **shared B matrices**, it totally nails this problem, making multimodal task performance skyrocket! ✨ So awesome! ['Paper Link'](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.05191) ['More Details'](https://gewu-lab.github.io/MokA)

### Social Media Buzz

1.  Renowned AI scholar **Gary Marcus** is at it again, dropping truth bombs! 🤔 Citing papers from **MIT, the University of Chicago, and Harvard**, he flat-out states that pure **LLMs** simply can't build **Artificial General Intelligence** (**AGI**)! Why not? Because they suffer from "**Potemkin-style understanding**" (aka fake understanding) and **conceptual inconsistency**. Basically, AI might ace tests, but when it comes to truly understanding and applying concepts, its true colors show. Research also found that LLMs like **GPT-4o**, after concepts are clearly defined, see their performance plummet 📉 when applied to real-world tasks like classification, generation, and editing. They even have **conflicting internal representations** of the same idea. This has sparked widespread attention and testing from industry bigwigs like **Google DeepMind scientist Prateek Jain**! Looks like the road to AGI for AI is still a long one! 💡 ['More Details'](https://www.jiqizhixin.com/articles/2025-06-29-5)
