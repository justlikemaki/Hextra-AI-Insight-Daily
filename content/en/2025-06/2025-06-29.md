### AI Frontier Research
1.  Google DeepMind has launched **AlphaGenome**🧬🔬, a groundbreaking "**gene-understanding AI**" model. It can accurately predict how variations in DNA's **non-coding regions** affect gene regulation, which is a huge boost for disease mechanism research and synthetic biology. It blows existing tech out of the water when it comes to handling super long **DNA sequences** and predicting regulatory properties, and its API is already open for non-commercial research use. Paper: ['https://deepmind.google/discover/blog/alphagenome-ai-for-better-understanding-the-genome/'](https://deepmind.google/discover/blog/alphagenome-ai-for-better-understanding-the-genome/)

2.  🚀 A cutting-edge research effort from teams including Northeastern University, Chinese University of Hong Kong, and Adobe Research has introduced **DraftAttention**, a **video diffusion model acceleration method**!
